About this Guide

Hammer & Frisbee · our two little ones

Thank you so much for stopping by to look after our kitties! This little booklet walks you through getting into the house, food & water, and the litter boxes — plus a few small notes that make their day easier.

If anything is unclear or you run into trouble, please text or call us anytime. Phone numbers are at the bottom of this page.

Getting In

🔑 Entering the House

Passcode entry · Yale smart lock

We'll send you the door passcode via a secured message before your first visit. Just enter it on the keypad to unlock the front door.

🔒 After each drop-in visit, please press the "Yale" button on the lock to lock the door. One press locks it — a quick double-check before you walk away is always appreciated.

Litter Care

🚽 Litter Boxes

Inside the white cabinet · push fully back · 1:1 tofu + clay top-up

Litter box · tucked inside the white cabinet

The litter box is located inside the white cabinet. After cleaning, please push it all the way back in before closing the door — otherwise the kitties will not be able to enter it.

🧹 Cleaning & Top-up

  • When the litter gets low, please top it up. We usually mix Pidan tofu litter with clay litter in a 1 : 1 ratio
  • Scoop into the small trash bin next to the litter box
  • Once the bag is full, tie it off and drop it in the black trash bin on the north side of the house
  • Fresh trash bags are stocked in the spot shown below
📝 Note: If we've run out of Pidan tofu litter, just use the clay litter on its own — that's totally fine. The clay-litter brand in the photo may be outdated; whatever brand is on the shelf at the time is the right one to use.
